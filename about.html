ABOUT US

A L Jain Catering, led by Lohith Jain, is a trusted name in the catering industry, renowned for delivering authentic and delicious South and North Indian vegetarian cuisine. Located in Nagendra Block near Martin Luther Public School, Banashankari, Bengaluru, the business proudly serves a variety of clients for weddings, religious functions, corporate events, and family gatherings. With a strong focus on quality, hygiene, and traditional Jain values, A L Jain Catering ensures every meal is prepared with utmost care and devotion. Their commitment to excellence is reflected in their motto “Jai Jinendra” and in the spiritual undertone of their service.

As specialist catering contractors, A L Jain Catering offers an extensive menu that caters to diverse tastes while honoring dietary preferences rooted in Jain and Indian traditions. Whether it’s a lavish banana leaf thali or a carefully curated buffet spread, their team delivers a wholesome culinary experience. The inclusion of both North and South Indian dishes allows clients to enjoy a rich blend of flavors and regional delicacies, making every occasion memorable. Their personalized approach, quality ingredients, and commitment to satisfaction make them a preferred choice for vegetarian catering in Bengaluru.
